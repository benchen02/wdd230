Target Audience

As stated on my home page, my target audience would be those that either live in or are visiting the San Gabriel Valley and want a bit of local information of where to go or places to eat.

Personas

Eva Wong

Eva Wong is an young adult asian american that has moved to the San Gabriel Valley because of her job as an real estate agent. This website will be useful for her as it'll give her great information about the area and would be able to give clients a rough idea on what the local sceneary looks like.

Jack Michael

Jack Michael is an older gentleman that loves to travel. He's from the east coast and is slowly making his way to the west cost. He's been to various states and has made use to google to help him plan out his trip. He usually explores the locale himself so he would like some information about the places he visits.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Eva Wong is trying to sell to a prospective client in selling a house however the house is next to a busy street so can get kind of loud. But because she looked up using the San Gabriel Chamber of Commerce site, she found places that were near by and showed the client how convienent it was for them to find someplace to eat or the recreational events near by. And so she managed to convince the client that having a bit of noise is worth the cost of living in near such convienences.

Scenario 2

Jack Michael is planning a trip down to the San Gabriel Valley and is wanting to know what are some of the historical sites that he could visit in the valley. Maybe also find some place to eat in the mean time. He would also like to know how the weather is like so that he can pack for the apporiate clothing. The San Gabriel Chamber of Commerece site is prefect as it has everything he needs to know.

Scenario 3

Eva Wong has a client that is looking for a place to open up a restraunt. The client is relatively new to the area and Eva sugguests that he should join the San Gabriel Chamber of Commerece as there he can promote his business and hopefully attract client to his restraunt.

Scenario 4

Jack Michael is just passing through the San Gabriel Valley but it's getting close night time and is looking for a place to eat and stay for the night. He looks through the San Gabriel Chamber of Commerce site to find a decent place to eat and stay for a night. Not looking for much but the site will give him trustworthy business to look up.

Scenario 5

Eva Wong has a client that loves photgraphy and is currently looking for a studio near a park. She looks through the San Gabriel Chamber of Commerece site to see which parks may interest her client and help them find a place that isn't too far to the client's preference. She may also want to look up some histrocal sights just so that her client can have some options with thier photgraphy.
