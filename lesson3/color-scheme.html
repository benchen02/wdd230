Color Scheme

This will be my color scheme:

Primary Secondary Accent 1 Accent 2

#009845 #6ba539 #c5e384 #f0eea3

Here's how the color scheme is going to be used:

Primary will be used to most in the headings, sections, and footer
Secondary will be used for the background color of sections
Accent 1 will be used for section headings
Accent 2 will be used for the body background

Simple, but I think it looks good
