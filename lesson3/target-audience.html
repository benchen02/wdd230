Target Audience Description

I want my target audience to be those that are either looking to open up their own business or are looking to grow them. I will also target potential investors and bankers so that local business may have a starting point in trying to establish their own network and expand from there. I want both new and veteran businesses to have a chance at establishing and growing their businesses.

Personas

Gabriela

Gabriela is an early 20's entrepereneur looking for the perfect place to open up her business however doesn't have the resources to do so. She's also looking for an area where the competition isn't that high and so her business will have room to grow. She also needs a bit of help getting started as she has the knoweldge to run her business, but doesn't have the resources to do so. So she might need a bit of help.

John Johnson

John Johnson is a long time resident and investor in the Mountain Valley. He has a decent network of other business owners and a group of investor friends that trust in his judgement when it comes to investing in a new businesses. He often checks the chamber of commerce site to see if he can spot any new promising business that need a little bit of help in order to establish themselves.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Gabriela is looking to open up her business and is wanting to look for a place with very little competition so she decides to visit the Mountain Valley Chamber of Commerce site in order to see what business there are. She sees that there aren't alot of places selling the products that she's wanting to sell, so decides the location of her business and gets to work researching the area and lease prices in the area.

Scenario 2

Gabriela has now bought and done renovations to the building she just bought and is now ready to open for business. At first, business is slow, but because of the low competition, it starts very slowly picking up business. With that said, she noticed that even with business picking up slowly it wasn't really busy and at most only had a few customers at a time so decided to look at and join in the chamber of commerce for The Mountain Valley and look for connections and start networking from there.

Scenario 3

John Johnson was looking for his next big investment. He decided to one day just browse through the local business on the chamber site to see if anything caught is attention. As he was scrolling through the site, he made a list of a few businesses that caught his attention that were either looking for help or just wanted to expand.

Scenario 4

John Johnson was looking to expand his network this time around and saw that a few businesses opening up. Some he saw were already established and were just expanding, others however sounded new. So, he spent the rest of the week meeting these new business owners and giving them an opportunity to establish a network themselves using his own network as a starting point.

Scenario 5

Gabriela now has a successful buisiness in the area thanks to resources that the chamber site gave her. She now has her own network and is now helping other business owners that are just starting and giving them advice on what to do. She, like John Johnson, are giving them an opportunity to establish and grow their own network using the site and her own network as a basis.
