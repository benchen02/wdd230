Target Audience

My target audience would be people that are wanting to incorporate weather into thier planning as well as people that may be interested in top restraunts and the local news.

Personas

Persona 1

Lily is a 22 year old foodie and blogger. She started a blog about the restraunts she went to at 16. Then she was just following her parents around as they loved to go out and eat and so she started a blog to review these restraunts. Her blog started small but quickly gained traction and now people look for her reviews to see where to eat. Becausee of this, she got picked up by a magazine company so now travels quite a bit so she can experience and critique the local restraunts.

Person 2

Mike is an 30 year old outdoorsy type of person. The kind of person that loves going camping, beaches, and the likes. He also loves playing sports casually with his friends and is usually the main planner of road trips. He runs an online business of selling sports gear and camping tools. But since he's loves the out doors, it would be nice to plan for weather and know what are some good local restraunts he can stop by during his road trips.

Scenarios

Scenerio 1

Lily is planning another trip to a small town. When she arrives there she would like to know what the local weather like during her stay. She logs into our site to see that a new restraunt in the town is getting great reviews and so goes to check it out.

Scenerio 2

Lily is stuck in traffic and so wants to know what happened. She logs on and finds that there has been a major accedent up ahead. So she calls in her boss and tells him the situation. His boss is understanding as it's been snowing heavily lately. Though the snowing should stop within the next weeks.

Scenerio 3

Mike is wanting to plan for another road trip as he recently got a big order done and has the time and money to spare. When he plans this trip he wants to know how the weather will be in the next days at their locations as well as what restraunts are good at the locale.

Scenerio 4

Mike is planning on playing ball with his friends after they get off from work. However, looking at the website, they may want to postpone or cancel is the weather states that it's going to rain tonight and tomorrow. Maybe a nice camping trip out to the desert for the weekend might be a better plan.

Scenerio 5

Lily is concerned about a local shooting that happened yesterday. Luckly, everyone she knew was safe, but she still wanted to keep up to date about the event. So she logs in and sees that the suspect of the shooting has been caught and arrested, easing her mind that it's safer to go to work now.
