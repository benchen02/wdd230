What We Do

We are a small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods mainly fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area and on occastion, use national and international suppliers. We do cater to walk in traffic at our storefront in Carlsbad and, of course, food trucks across the west coast. We also deliver on orders either over the phone or our website.

Locations

We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, however, we have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego.

Mission

Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas. We also support healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience.
