What We Do

We are a small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. We're physically located in Carlsbad, California and have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. We sell fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, but also use some national and international suppliers. We also caters to walk in traffic at our storefront in Carlsbad and, of course, our food trucks. We deliver and manage those orders on the phone and via the website.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas. We also support healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience.

A Little Bit About Carlsbad

Carlsbad is located north of San Diego, Carlsbad lies along a lagoon on the Pacific Ocean just south of Oceanside, in a winter vegetable- and flower-growing district. Founded as a cattle station located along the Spanish Camino Real (royal highway) and settled by Anglo ranchers in the late 1860s, it was first called Frazier's Station but was renamed in 1883, when its mineral waters were found to be similar to those of Karlsbad in Bohemia (now Karlovy Vary, Czech Republic). After the arrival of the Santa Fe Railway in 1885, the city's fine beaches spurred its development as a popular health resort and vacation destination. Our Histroy

Founded in 1992, we wanted a place where people could get fresh products from local sources and so we started a farmer's market. After a while, farmers found success as their products were fresh and were drawing in new people from all over the coast and decided to try and expand their market. They decided to send their products to near by cities to advertise and eventually found success in selling fresh fruits and vegetables in those near by cities. We now sell fresh fruits and produce across the Southern California Coast, all mostly fresh from local sources so that we may support our local farmers, who bring in fresh, quality products.
